The history of the internet

Origins

Before the internet, networks existed as isolated islands…

The ARPANET project, funded by the U.S. Department of Defense, sought to connect those islands.

Standardization

TCP/IP became the lingua franca of inter-network communication in the early 1980s…

The transition off the older NCP protocol took years to complete.

The Web

The World Wide Web emerged from CERN in 1991…

Hypertext made navigation between documents trivially easy.

Early browsers

Mosaic, then Netscape, then Internet Explorer…

Commerce

The dot-com boom of the late 1990s brought the web to a mass audience…

Mobile

The iPhone, in 2007, made the web pocket-sized…

Today

Modern protocols, video, voice, and persistent encryption…
